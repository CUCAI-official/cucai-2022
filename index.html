FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions
If you have any further questions, feel free to reach out to us at chair@cucai.ca
When is the conference taking place?
CUCAI 2021 is taking place online the weekend of April 2nd and 3rd. A full schedule detailing times and events happening on both days will be announced soon.
How selective is the application process?
The application process this year is decided to be as inclusive as possible, in keeping with our mission to increase the accessibility of AI education at an undergraduate level. All applications that show a genuine effort and sincere passion for learning about AI will be accepted.
How expensive are tickets?
As we are vitrual this year, the conference will be available remotely for $10, which includes your complementary food voucher. Please note you must apply in oder to gain access to your ticket purchase.
Will the conference be virtual or in-person?
Due to the continuation of Covid-19 restrictions, we have decided to take CUCAI virtual. We strive to keep our conference of the highest quality while keeping all attendees safe.
Do I need to be a part of a university club to attend?
CUCAI is open to all undergraduate students across Canada. Many of our delegates are associated with a university AI group, but we encourage all students to attend. If you wish to submit a design project but are not associated with a student group, fill out your application and we will reach out to you!
Can I attend the conference as a graduate student?
CUCAI is geared towards undergraduate students, however we strive to be as inclusive as possible. If you are not an undergraduate student but still wish to attend CUCAI 2022, please feel free to contact delegates@cucai.ca and we will get back to you shortly.
